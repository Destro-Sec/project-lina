    About
    Project L.I.N.A ( Laterally Integrated Neural Architecture ) is a nonprofit R&D organization working on ethical advancements in artificial intelligence technology and it's use cases within various cross industry domains of practice.
    Advocating for progressive and reasonable regulations within the AI space. Developing, deploying, operating and maintaining custom AI models, while simultaneously publishing relevant theoretical esearch within the space. Furthering other holistic and social welfare oriented ventures and initiatives to increase efficiency of value production and quality of human life

    About Us

    Where Innovation Meets Artificial Intelligence

    Welcome to Project L.I.N.A the home of new gen revolution in AI Technology. Our journey began with a dream to make sure that AI implementation is executed in an ethical & unbiased manner and that it does more good than harm, resulting in, hopefully an overwhelming net positive, instead of the matrix-like dystopia that we're currently portrayed to be headed towards.

    L.I.N.A Tech - Functioning as the primary R&D wing, LT focuses on progressive and integrative research and implementation of augmentative AI.


    LT works according to a set of proprietary principles, championing an ethics-first and user-oriented approach that prioritizes user privacy and private data security.

    L.I.N.A Tech

    LINA Corp - Functioning as the main corporate wing, It aims to foster a collaborative spirit within the AI development space within India, and extend it further abroad. LC also provides Business Development Services and Assistance to other startups and innovative teams working on related domains.


    LC further champions the ethical implentation of AI in other industry niches, and works towards furthering publishing and promotion of ground breaking innovations in AI technology and their practical development and en masse deployment for public benefit.

    L.I.N.A Corp

    LINA Foundation - Functioning as the central welfare wing, It aims to foster an inclusive and mutually collaborative spirit within stakeholders in the AI space.


    LF further operates various targeted welfare initiatives aimed at promoting ethical and beneficial integration of AI into society alongside other causes aimed at creating a welfare state of affairs in the tech economy and society at large.
